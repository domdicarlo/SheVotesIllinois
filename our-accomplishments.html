She Votes Illinois Vice Board Member Sophia Madana (center) with Votes & Vino event attendees


Intro: Hear Our Voice!

Starting in 2017, She Votes Illinois has been on a mission to amplify women's voices, advocate for women's rights and get more women involved in politics in the state of Illinois.

And we’re not done yet. We are committed to highlighting the importance of women’s voices in elections, government, and policymaking. Join us in our pursuit.
